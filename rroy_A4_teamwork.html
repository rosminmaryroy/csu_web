Why Teamwork Matters at Verdant Web Solutions

A web agency runs on overlapping work: designers hand off mockups to developers, who hand off builds to QA, who hand off to the client. Someone who shows up reliably, communicates blockers early, and helps teammates over the line keeps the whole pipeline moving.

Examples of My Teamwork

CSU Group Project

  1. Co-led a four-person ITC project to design a small business website
  2. Volunteered to integrate everyone's pages into a single repository
  3. Set up a shared task list so nobody duplicated work

Spice Blend Front-of-House

  • Worked closely with kitchen staff on Friday-night service for over a year
  • Covered colleagues' shifts on short notice when family emergencies came up
  • Trained two new hires on the booking system using a one-page cheat sheet I wrote

Volunteering — Local Community Centre

I help once a month at a community IT-help session, partnered with two other students. We rotate who answers the door, who logs questions, and who actually fixes the laptop — and we always finish on time because we agreed the rotation beforehand.

Team belief: plan together, deliver together.
