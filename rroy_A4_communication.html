Why Communication Matters at Verdant Web Solutions

Verdant Web Solutions builds custom websites for small businesses across Australia. Translating a client's vague vision into a clear technical plan — and then back into non-technical updates — is the heart of every project. Strong communication is what turns a one-off build into a long-term client relationship.

Client-Facing Practice

While working part-time at Spice Blend, I handled phone bookings and walk-in enquiries in two languages (English and Malayalam). I learned to listen first, repeat the request back to confirm understanding, and only then commit to an answer. That habit transfers directly to gathering web project requirements.

Communication style: clear, concise, client-first.
